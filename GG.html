Georgia Gkioxari
georgia.gkioxari@gmail.com

I am a research scientist at FAIR. I received my PhD from UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Jitendra Malik. I did my bachelors in ECE at NTUA in Athens, Greece, where I worked with Petros Maragos.

In the past, I have spent time at Google Brain and Google Research, where I worked with Navdeep Jaitly and Alexander Toshev.
